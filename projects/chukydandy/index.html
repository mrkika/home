About Hon. Festus Okoh

Hon. Festus Okoh, popularly known as Chuky Dandy, is a distinguished lawmaker representing Ika South Constituency in the Delta State House of Assembly. A passionate advocate for development, empowerment, and good governance, he has served his people with unwavering dedication and transparency.

With years of experience in legislative work and grassroots development, Hon Chuky Dandy has spearheaded numerous initiatives that have uplifted his constituency. His leadership is anchored on progress, inclusion, and integrity.

Political Career

A journey of service, growth, and transformation

2007

Grassroots Leadership

Began his political career by working with local leaders and youth groups to promote community empowerment.

2011

Political Debut

Ventured into politics by contesting for the House of Representatives seat for the Ika Federal Constituency under the Democratic People‚Äôs Party (DPP). Although unsuccessful, this marked his entry into the political arena.

2015

First Electoral Victory

Elected to the Delta State House of Assembly representing Ika South Constituency on the platform of the Accord Party. He was appointed Minority Leader of the House.

2017

Party Defection

Defected from the Accord Party to the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), citing internal crises within his former party.

2018

PDP Re-Election Bid

Declared his intention to seek re-election and purchased the PDP nomination form.

2019

Second Term Victory

Won the PDP primary election for Ika South Constituency, securing the party's ticket for the 2019 general elections and Re-elected to the Delta State House of Assembly representing Ika South Constituency under the PDP.

2023

Elevated Leadership Role

Appointed Deputy Majority Leader in the 8th Delta State House of Assembly in June 2023.

Today

Serving With Vision

Continues to legislate with passion, focusing on infrastructure, social welfare, and youth development across Delta State.
