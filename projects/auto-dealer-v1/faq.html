Frequently Asked Questions

What is My Website?

My Website is a trusted auto auction company based in United Kingdom. We specialize in selling high-quality vehicles at competitive prices through our transparent auction process.

How do I participate in an auction?

You can participate in an auction by registering on our website and submitting the required documentation. Once registered, you can place bids on available vehicles during live auctions.

Do I need to pay a deposit to bid?

Yes, a refundable deposit is required to secure your participation in the auction. The deposit ensures the integrity of the bidding process.

Are the vehicles inspected before being auctioned?

Yes, all vehicles undergo a thorough inspection before being listed for auction. Detailed inspection reports are available for each vehicle.

Can I view the vehicles before bidding?

Yes, we encourage bidders to visit our premises and inspect the vehicles in person during the preview days scheduled before each auction.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers and major credit cards. Full payment must be made within 48 hours after winning the bid.

Is there a buyer’s fee?

Yes, a buyer’s fee is applicable. The fee percentage will be disclosed before the auction starts.

What happens if I win a bid but fail to pay?

Failure to complete the payment within the stipulated time will result in the forfeiture of your deposit and suspension from future auctions.
